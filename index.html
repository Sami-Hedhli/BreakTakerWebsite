Save yourself from RSI and eye-strain

BreakTaker allows you to set up customizable breaks and will remind you to take them.
BreakTaker is free and open source and available for Windows, macOS and Linux.

Star

Configure your schedule

BreakTaker allows you to configure how often and how long your breaks should be.

Customize your experience

Choose the app colours and messages that are shown during breaks. You can also fetch a famous quote.

Set working hours

Ensure you are only interrupted when you actually want to be by configuring working hours.

Notifications

BreakTaker let's you know when breaks are about to start.

Idle reset(in progress)

BreakTaker can intelligently restart your break countdown when it detects that you have not been at your computer.
